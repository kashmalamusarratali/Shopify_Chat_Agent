Welloff Collection – Return & Exchange Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Quality & Confidence

At Welloff Collection, we believe that luxury should feel effortless. Every garment is crafted with intention, precision tailoring, and premium fabrics designed to elevate everyday wear. However, we understand that fit, texture, or personal preference may not always align perfectly.

This Return & Exchange Policy outlines how returns, refunds, store credits, and exchanges are handled for purchases made directly through WelloffCollection.com.

By completing a purchase, you agree to the terms outlined below.

2. 21-Day Return Window

We offer a 21-day return period from the date your order is marked as delivered.

To qualify for a return:

The return request must be initiated within 21 calendar days of delivery.

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and unused.

All original tags must remain attached.

Items must be returned in original packaging.

Garments must be free of stains, fragrance, deodorant marks, or alterations.

Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required.

Returns initiated after 21 days may not be accepted.

3. Eligible Items

The following items are eligible for return under standard conditions:

Tailored blazers

Trousers

Shirts & blouses

Knitwear

Outerwear

Dresses

Accessories (excluding earrings and intimate items)

Items must be in resellable condition.

4. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene, customization, and limited-run production reasons, the following are non-returnable:

Final Sale or Clearance items

Personalized or monogrammed garments

Tailored-to-measure pieces

Undergarments

Swimwear (if protective liner removed)

Gift cards

If you receive a defective final sale item, contact support within 48 hours.

5. Exchanges

We offer complimentary exchanges within the United States for:

Size adjustments

Color swaps

Same-style replacements

Exchange inventory is not reserved until the return package is scanned by the carrier.

If exchanging for a higher-priced item, the price difference must be paid.
If lower-priced, store credit will be issued for the balance.

International exchanges require the customer to cover return shipping.

6. Return Shipping Fees
Domestic Orders (United States)

$9 flat return shipping fee deducted from refund.

Free exchanges.

Free returns for defective or incorrect items.

International Orders

Customer responsible for return shipping.

Original shipping fees and customs duties are non-refundable.

We recommend insured, trackable shipping for international returns.

7. Refund Processing

Once your return is received:

Inspection takes 3–5 business days.

Refund is issued within 5 business days after approval.

Bank processing may take 3–10 business days.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

Shipping charges are non-refundable.

8. Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or defective:

Email support@welloffcollection.com
 within 48 hours.

Provide clear photos of the issue.

Include your order number.

We may offer:

Replacement

Full refund

Partial refund

Store credit

Welloff Collection covers all shipping costs for defective merchandise.

9. Return Rejection

Returns may be denied if:

Items show signs of wear.

Tags are removed.

Items have odors or visible stains.

Garments have been altered.

Items are returned outside the return window.

Rejected items will be shipped back at the customer’s expense.

10. Holiday Extension Policy

Orders placed between November 15 and December 24 may be returned through January 15.

11. Excessive Return Monitoring

We reserve the right to:

Monitor excessive return behavior

Restrict future returns

Suspend accounts engaging in policy abuse

12. Policy Modifications

Welloff Collection reserves the right to update this policy at any time.
