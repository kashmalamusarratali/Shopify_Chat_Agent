Welloff Collection – Shipping & Delivery Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days (Monday–Friday, excluding holidays).

During peak seasons, processing times may extend to 3–5 business days.

Orders are not processed on weekends or public holidays.

2. Shipping Options
United States

Standard Shipping (3–7 business days)

Expedited Shipping (2–3 business days)

Express Shipping (1–2 business days)

Free standard shipping is available on domestic orders over $150.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide.

Estimated delivery time:

7–14 business days (varies by country)

International customers are responsible for:

Customs duties

Import taxes

Brokerage fees

Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive:

Shipping confirmation email

Tracking number

Carrier information

Tracking updates may take 24–48 hours to reflect movement.

4. Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for entering accurate shipping information.

Welloff Collection is not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by customers.

If an order is returned due to incorrect address:

Customer must pay reshipping fees.

Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

5. Lost or Delayed Packages

If tracking shows no movement for 5+ business days:

Contact support.

We will initiate a carrier investigation.

Replacement or refund may be issued at our discretion.

We are not responsible for carrier delays due to weather, customs, or peak season demand.

6. Split Shipments

Orders containing multiple items may ship separately if:

Items are stored in different warehouses.

One item is backordered.

You will receive separate tracking numbers.

7. Refused Shipments

If a shipment is refused:

Return shipping costs will be deducted from your refund.

Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

8. Signature Requirement

High-value orders may require a signature upon delivery.

9. Risk of Loss

Ownership transfers to the customer once the carrier confirms delivery.

10. Policy Updates

Welloff Collection may modify shipping policies at any time.
